'Group' corresponds to the structure of a complex type.

'Element' corresponds to a element within a complex type. Its types include:
int, double, string, intlist, doublelist, stringlist, complex, complexlist, boolean, booleanlist, timestamp, table

All 'Name's consist of letters, numbers and '_', beginning with upper letter.
